About the Departmental Board

The Departmental Board is a central platform created for CIT 306 Web Development Project. It serves as an information hub designed to make departmental communication more accessible, organized, and student-friendly.

Our Purpose

The board was built to provide announcements, events, timetables, and results in a single digital space, reducing the stress of searching for updates across multiple channels.

Key Features

  • Announcements: Latest news and departmental updates.
  • Events: View upcoming seminars and meetings.
  • Timetables: Access class schedules with ease.
  • Results: Check uploaded results securely.

Our Vision

We aim to build a more connected departmental community by leveraging technology to deliver timely information, encourage participation, and foster a collaborative environment.
